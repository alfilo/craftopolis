About Craftopolis

Hello! My name is Alina. Last year, I participated in girl scouts and 4H and we did a lot of crafting projects. So, I thought I’d like to sell some at the Lansing Artisan Fair. I made some crafts from patterns, and some I made up on my own.

My goal is not only to sell crafts, but also to inspire others to make their own. Many of the crafts I don't sell turn into teaching tools for 4-H, or I give them away over the holidays as presents.

All funds raised will go toward buying beads and supplies for next year’s artisan fair, and any profit will be put towards raising money for college. Hope you find something inspiring at this year's Lansing Artisan Fair!
