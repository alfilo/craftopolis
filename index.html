About Craftopolis

Hello! My name is Alina. The last few years, I participated in 4-H and we did a lot of crafting projects. So, I thought I’d like to start my own business selling the crafts I make. Some crafts I make from patterns, and many are my own original designs. The crafts I make are often exhibited at the Tompkins County 4-H Fair and New York State Fair.

My goal is not only to sell crafts, but to inspire others to make their own. Many of the crafts that I don't sell become teaching tools for 4-H, or I give them away throughout the season as presents.

All funds raised will go toward buying beads and supplies for next year’s sales, and any profit will be put towards raising money for college or community service projects. Hope you find something inspiring at this year's sale!
